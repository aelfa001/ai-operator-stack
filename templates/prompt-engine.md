## 1. Business Writing

### Cold Email Generator
```
Write a cold email to {{prospect_name}} at {{company}} about {{your_service}}.

Rules:
- Keep it under 150 words
- Start with a specific observation about THEIR business
- Show you understand their pain: {{main_pain}}
- One clear CTA
- No attachments mentioned
- Sound like a real person, not a sales robot

Write 3 variations, each with a different hook.
```

### Follow-up Email
```
Write a follow-up email for someone who {{action_taken}} but hasn't responded yet.

Context:
- Original email was about: {{topic}}
- Last contact was {{days}} days ago
- Their role is {{role}}

Keep it short, add value, make it easy to say "not interested" if that's true.
```

### Proposal Outline
```
Create a proposal outline for {{project_type}} project for {{client_name}}.

- Proposed solution
- Timeline (approx {{duration}})
- Investment options (3 tiers)
- Next steps

Make it scannable with clear headings.
```

## 4. Sales & Outreach

### Qualifying Questions
```
Generate 5 discovery questions for a prospect interested in {{service}}.

Include:
- 2 questions about their current situation
- 2 questions about their goals/challenges
- 1 question about budget/timeline

Format as a natural conversation, not an interrogation.
```

### Objection Handling
```
How do I respond when a prospect says: "{{objection}}"

Context:
- They {{what_they_said}}
- My typical price is {{price}}
- Their main concern seems to be {{concern}}

Write 3 different responses:
1. Acknowledge and address directly
2. Reframe the value
3. Use social proof

Keep each under 50 words.
```

### Re-engagement Email
```
Write an email to re-engage a past client: {{client_name}}

Context:
- Last worked together: {{date}}
- Their business has likely changed: {{assumption}}
- New capability I have: {{new_offer}}

Make it about them, not me selling. Offer value first.
```

### Sales Email Sequence
```
Create a 5-email sequence for {{offer}}.

Email 1: Hook - {{hook_idea}}
Email 2: Social proof - {{case_story}}
Email 3: Value breakdown - {{key_benefits}}
Email 4: Objection handling - {{common_objection}}
Email 5: Last chance - {{urgency_element}}

Each email: Subject line + body. Short and punchy.
```

### Pricing Email
```
Write an email to {{client}} explaining pricing for {{project}}.

Pricing: {{amount}}
Payment terms: {{terms}}

Include:
- What's included
- Why it's priced this way
- What's not included
- How to proceed
- Alternative options if needed

Be confident, not apologetic.
```

### Getting Better Results:
1. **Be specific** - More context = better output
2. **Add constraints** - "Under 200 words" or "In a casual tone"
3. **Ask for variations** - "Give me 3 options"
4. **Iterate** - "Make this more X" or "Simplify this"
